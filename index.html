Focusing on the Taiwan region, I utilize numerical methods to simulate complex ocean currents and wave interactions. My work involves processing large datasets from CMEMS and CWB to understand regional hydrodynamics. The goal is to map coarse global data to high-resolution local models for better predictability. Computational Fluid Dynamics (CFD)

My expertise includes working with OpenFOAM and the Volume of Fluid (VOF) method to estimate curvature and track interfaces. I actively investigate non-linear thin-film equations and turbulence stability, writing custom solvers in Python and MATLAB to troubleshoot numerical schemes and CFL conditions.

Hybrid Deep Learning Frameworks

I am developing a hybrid framework integrating Singular Value Decomposition (SVD) with Multi-Layer Perceptrons (MLP) and Residual U-Net correction. This novel approach targets oceanographic data super-resolution, significantly enhancing the accuracy of ocean variable predictions compared to traditional interpolation methods.

Geospatial Image Analysis

Using techniques like LiDAR point cloud processing and drone orthophotos, I create accurate ground elevation maps for environmental monitoring. Recently, I have also begun exploring the application of neural networks to biological data for pattern detection and cancer research analysis.
